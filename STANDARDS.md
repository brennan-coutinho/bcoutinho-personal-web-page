# Standards Document

## 01 — Project Overview

This project is a personal professional landing page for Brennan Coutinho, a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa graduating in December 2026.

The purpose of this site is to complement a traditional resume by showcasing Brennan’s personal story, technical skills, and project experience in a more engaging and accessible format. It highlights his journey as an international student who switched majors and developed a strong interest in data analytics, particularly in identifying patterns and asking meaningful questions.

The site is designed for recruiters and hiring managers evaluating candidates for entry-level Data Analyst and Business Analyst roles.

---

## 02 — Technical Approach

- The site is built as a **single-page website**
- Developed using:
  - HTML for structure
  - CSS for styling
- No frameworks or libraries are used
- All files are stored locally and organized in a simple folder structure

---

## 03 — Design Standards

- The site uses a clean and simple layout for readability
- Content is organized into clear sections:
  - Hero
  - Bio
  - Skills
  - Projects
  - Contact
- Headings are used to separate sections
- Basic spacing is applied between sections for clarity
- Simple animations are used (fade-in effect) to enhance user experience without distraction
- The design prioritizes clarity and usability over complexity
